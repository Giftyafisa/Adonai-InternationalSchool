A Message from the Principal

Welcome to Adonai International School. As we celebrate a decade of academic excellence, I am filled with immense pride in what our community has accomplished together. From our humble beginnings to becoming a cornerstone of education in our region, our journey has been guided by a steadfast commitment to our students.

Our motto, "Discipline and Hardwork," is not just a phrase; it is the foundation upon which our institution is built. We believe that true success requires character development alongside academic rigor. We strive to create an environment where every child feels valued, supported, and challenged to reach their highest potential.

Thank you to our dedicated staff, supportive parents, and incredible students for making Adonai a place of continuous learning and growth. The next decade promises even greater innovations as we continue to shape the leaders of tomorrow.

Dr. Emmanuel Mensah

Principal, Adonai International School

Our Heritage & Home

Rooted in the community of Adjei Kojo, growing towards a global future.

history_edu A Decade of Growth

Established ten years ago, Adonai International School began with a clear vision: to provide accessible, high-quality education that blends traditional values with modern pedagogical approaches. What started with a small cohort of eager learners has blossomed into a thriving educational institution.

Located in the vibrant community of Adjei Kojo, our campus has expanded significantly over the years. We have continually upgraded our facilities to include state-of-the-art science laboratories, a comprehensive library, and modern sports complexes to ensure a well-rounded educational experience.
